How MedTrack Works

We Have a delightful user experience

Register

Start your journey by creating an account or using guest mode — no stress, no barriers. Your health tracking starts instantly.

Set Up Your Medication

Add your medications, dosage, and how often you take them. You can also input how many pills you currently have for refill tracking

Customize your Reminder

Choose when you want to be notified — morning, afternoon, night or custom times. The reminders are gentle and consistent.

Stay on Track With Smart Alerts

Receive timely reminders and log each dose as Taken, Remind Me Later, or Skip. Missed doses trigger smart alerts to keep you consistent.

Track Progress And Refill easily

View your medication history, streaks, and refill alerts when your stock runs low. MedTrack keeps you organized and in control.
